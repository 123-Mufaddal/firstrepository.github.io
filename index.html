About Us

Nisa Hospital cares for women 24x7, addressing their individual medical needs at each stage in their lives. From personalized prenatal care and neonatal care to helping them cope with the physical and mental effects of menopause, we have been caring for women and the newborns, since we first established our medical facility in 1989. Specializing in a wide range of gynecological care services, Nisa Hospital offers far more to women, such as Cancer Screening, Gynecological Surgery, Laparoscopic Surgery , General Surgery and additional neonatal services.
